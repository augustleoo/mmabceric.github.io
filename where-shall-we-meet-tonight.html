WHERE SHALL WE MEET TONIGHT?

Sexual desires and fantasies are mostly unspoken in public but lived out in digital space. In this new space we find comfort and freedom but also loneliness when it comes to the topic of sex and pleasure. 30% of all data transferred through the Internet is porn-related but the topic is rarely touched or discussed in everyday life.

這是2018年跨文化合作項目中，我與Claudio RAINOLTER、張靖瑩和劉思佳共同創作的作品。

展出於香港視覺藝術院。

今晚哪裡見？

性幻想通常隱身于日常空間卻被大量製造在網路空間。與此同時，虛擬空間正不斷擴張，合併重疊于人們的日常。游移在虛擬的體感與真實的想象之間，孤獨在高潮之後，自由在界線之外。我們希望探討現實的凹面如何在此顯影，是如何在此之間，又是如何在此之外。

A collaboration work with Claudio RAINOLTER, Jingying ZHANG and Sijia LIU, during Transcultural Collaboration 2018.

Presented in Academy of Visual Arts, Hong Kong.
