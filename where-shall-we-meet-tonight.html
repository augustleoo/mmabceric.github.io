words

WHERE SHALL WE MEET TONIGHT?

In the context of global mobility, home is everywhere, yet where is the real home?

Maybe you could find the answer in this space that had only existed for one day and unceasingly traveled between the memory and the present, plus the virtual and the real.

今晚哪裡見？

在全球化流動的語境中，家無處不在，而何處是家？

 

在這個只存在一天，不斷穿梭于回憶和當下、虛擬和現實的空間里，也許你能找到答案。

 

 

 

 

這是2018年跨文化合作項目中，我與Claudio RAINOLTER、張靖瑩和劉思佳共同創作的作品。

展出於香港視覺藝術院。

A collaboration work with Claudio RAINOLTER, Jingying ZHANG and Sijia LIU, during Transcultural Collaboration 2018.

Presented in Academy of Visual Arts, Hong Kong.
