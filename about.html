LIAO JIAMING

廖家明

 

 

 

 

 

 

LIAO JIAMING is an artist who works with images.

His work focus on life and population in urban cities, especially sexual minority related issues.

 

He also writes about subtlety and ambiguity, with an over-sensitive mind of daily life and a seemingly ignorant attitude.

 

He obtained a Bachelor of Arts in Journalism from Sun Yat-sen University in 2016. He is now a Master of Fine Arts student in the School of Creative Media, City University of Hong Kong.

 

藝術家廖家明以圖像為媒介進行創作。

他的作品多關注都市人和城市生活，尤其性少數群體相關議題。

 

他也寫作，以對日常生活的過分敏感假裝若無其事地書寫細微和曖昧。

 

2016年，他從中山大學畢業并獲取新聞文學學士學位。

現在，他正在香港城市大學創意媒體學院攻讀藝術碩士課程。
